FEATURES GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

ALGORITHMS:
  • FCFS - First Come First Served (simple baseline)
  • SSTF - Shortest Seek Time First (locality optimization)
  • SCAN - Elevator algorithm (prevent starvation)
  • C-SCAN - Circular SCAN (uniform wait times)
  • LOOK - SCAN with lookahead (modern optimization)
  • C-LOOK - Circular LOOK (best overall)

PRESETS:
  • Heavy Load - 20 random requests (queue buildup test)
  • Clustered - 15 requests in 3 regions (locality test)
  • Random - 10 uniform requests (baseline)

VISUALIZATION:
  • 3D disk platter with track rings
  • Mechanical read/write head
  • Amber LED indicator for I/O activity
  • Real-time animation of head movement
  • Isometric camera view

METRICS:
  • Current track position
  • Total service time
  • Head movements count
  • Average seek distance
  • Average wait time
  • Queue status
